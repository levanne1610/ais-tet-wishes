About Us

Tết, the Vietnamese Lunar New Year, marks the arrival of spring and celebrates Vietnam's rich cultural heritage. Families come together to honor ancestors, prepare festive meals, decorate with symbolic plants, and exchange heartfelt wishes that embody the spirit of hope and renewal.

A Tết Wish symbolizes gratitude, blessings for health, happiness, and prosperity, and fosters unity and joy. This website provides a platform to share and preserve these meaningful wishes, connecting students and teacher in the spirit of Tết and celebrating its timeless traditions.

Why Sharing Wishes?

Symbol of Good Fortune

Sharing wishes at Tết represents hope for a prosperous year ahead. These wishes, often tied to health, success, and happiness, carry the belief that kind words and intentions can influence the year’s blessings, creating a sense of optimism and positivity among families, friends, and communities.

Strengthening Relationships

Tết wishes play a vital role in strengthening relationships, as they encourage meaningful exchanges among loved ones. By sharing heartfelt messages, people reconnect, express gratitude, and build stronger bonds. This act nurtures familial and social harmony, ensuring that the festive spirit is filled with warmth and togetherness.

Expression of Respect

Wishing elders and peers during Tết demonstrates profound respect and gratitude. It acknowledges the contributions of others in one’s life, reinforcing the value of mutual appreciation. This act strengthens familial hierarchy and cultural values, ensuring respect remains a cornerstone of Vietnamese traditions.

Fostering Positivity

Tết wishes spread joy and positivity, setting the tone for a harmonious year ahead. By exchanging optimistic words and blessings, people inspire hope and motivation. This practice uplifts spirits, encourages resilience, and creates an atmosphere of happiness, uniting everyone in the celebration of new beginnings.
